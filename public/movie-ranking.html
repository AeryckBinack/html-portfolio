The Best Movies Ever According to Eric

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

1. Apocalypse Now

An endlessly beautiful and fascinating film about war that goes beyond simple critique and develops grand mythologies and Wagnerian scope.

2. Troll 2

A terrible movie that's almost always hilarious and great for those bad-movie nights. I can't help it, the movie is amazingly funny.

3. Dead Alive

The best horror comedy of all time is almost Peter Jackson's true masterpiece. It works as horror and comedy in ways you don't see with other flicks.
